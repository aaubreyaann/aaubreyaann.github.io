Target Audience

Who: Men and women who live in, or will be visiting the three subject cities. They are small town people.

Age: Adults, ages 28-65

Technology: They have a variety of technology, including desktops, tablets and smart phones.

Income: between $40,500 and $90,000

Motivation: Their motivation is to get daily weather updates, as well as see the weather predictions for the near future.

Personas

James Farfield

  • Occupation: Local Grocery Store Owner
  • Demographics and Education: 60 years old. James has his bachelors in business management.
  • Goals and motivation for using the site: Check the local weather has he walks to his store each morning, as well as check the weather for the weekend in anticipation of his fishing trip.
  • Social: James likes sitting on his porch at home, as well as fishing on the lake in his free time with his brother.
  • Technology: James loves his desktop computer, and gets on it each morning to check news and event. He also uses Samsung Tablets at his store for convienent inventory and scanning purposes.
  • Quote:"A day spent inside is a day wasted."



Lilian McArthur

  • Occupation: Stay at home mom.
  • Demographics and Education: 32 years old. Associates in Marriage and Family Studies
  • Goals and motivation for using the site: Check the weather for barbeques with family, and in hopes that she can send her kids outside to play. She also checks to see the weather for her sun and his paper route.
  • Social: Morning jogs with neighborhood moms, neighborhood barbeques and weekly trips to the farmers market.
  • Technology: Lilian uses her desktop, smartphone, and has iPads that she shares with her children.
  • Quote:"Live, Laugh, Love."

Scenarios

1. Is this a good vaction spot?
2. Will I need a jacket as I walk to work?
3. What does my old home town look like?
4. Will the weather be good for me to spend a weekend at my lake house?
5. Can I have a barbeque outside tomorrow night?
