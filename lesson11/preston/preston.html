Winter Wonderland

by Laney Roe

This past winter was a dream for many in Preston. Snow was late to come, but when it came, everything was covered overnight. There was not a tree top left uncovered. This led to multiple snow days for the children, and parents had to get creative. Old farmer John was a great help during this wintery season. Every Saturday, he hooked up his horses to his buggy, and gave children sleigh rides through the snow to his field. Here he had created a spendid winter castle. Families came from near and far to see his creation. There were rooms galore, and icy staircases. One section even had mounds of snowballs with battle stations. This was definitely a winter to remember.
