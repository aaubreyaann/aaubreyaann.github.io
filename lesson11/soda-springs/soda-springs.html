Visitors at the Lake

by Mary Moe

In February of 2020, the Billybob family came to Soda Springs to visit their grandmother. Snow covered the ground for the first of their trip, so they spent their days playing in the snow in the backyard, and warming up inside by the fire. On their last day in Soda Springs, the snow had turned to slush on the ground. They decided that they wanted to take a walk down by the lake. They bundled up their kids, and head off to see the partially frozen lake. The lake was gorgeous. The rising sun glittered off of the ice and a rush of peace overcame the family. Out of no where the youngest exclaims, "Mommy! Look a penguin!" The Mother turns to see a penguin waddling around the corner. The whole family stares, baffled as what's going on. The penguin sqauks and they hear from behing the tall boulders, "Ming Ming, get back here this instant!" The family is once again baffled at the situation. A zoo keeper walks around the corner. It ended up being a zoo fieldtrip day, as the weather had become nice enough for the penguins to take a walk.
